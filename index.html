Why I created this page?

I have decided to start this page for two reasons.

One: I am amazed by the progress in the makers movement and how easy it is to build things nowadays. Also it is astounding how the software world is entering into traditional hardware like circuit breakers, switchgear assemblies or power generation sets.It seems to me that the modern Electrical Engineers (EE's) should be fluent in a programming language, which allows them solve complex engineering problems quicker.

Two: I am fairly worried to hear news from universities in Poland that less and less students decide to study electrical engineering. It gets me thinking how will I learn about new trends in science if not from fresh graduates that come to work with me.

I hope that thanks to this page I will be able to show that electrical engineering can also be cool and that at least some of people who will get a chance to read it will chose a career as an EE.
